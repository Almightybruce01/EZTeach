## Objection Handlers

### "We already use [competitor]"
> "I understand. What I'd love to show you is [highlight their pain point]. Most schools that switch save $X and consolidate Y systems."

### "Our teachers aren't tech-savvy"
> "We designed for simplicity. Free onboarding training included. Most teachers are comfortable in 1-2 days."

### "What about data privacy?"
> "Fully COPPA/FERPA compliant. Encrypted data, US servers, we never sell data."

### "Can we import existing data?"
> "Yes! CSV import for students/staff. Most schools complete import in under an hour."

### "What if it goes down?"
> "Built on Google Cloud with 99.9% uptime. Never had a major outage."

# EZTeach -- The All-in-One K-12 School Platform

## 50+ Features. 1 App.

EZTeach replaces the 10+ software subscriptions your school already pays for -- attendance, grades, messaging, lesson plans, videos, books, games, and more. One login. One bill. Everything works together.

---

## Features

**AI Lesson Plans** -- Generate full lesson plans in seconds, aligned to your state standards (CCSS, TEKS, NGSS, B.E.S.T., and more). Copy, paste, print.

**Dynamic Standards** -- National + state frameworks built in. Districts and schools can add overrides. Searchable Standards Explorer.

**AI Tutoring for Parents** -- AI creates personalized study plans parents can follow at home.

**120+ Educational Movies** -- Science, history, nature, animated, documentaries. No ads.

**Free Books Library** -- Thousands of free books. 100+ picture books with read-aloud. Multiplayer Read Together sessions. School Library with check-in/out.

**Learning Games and Leaderboards** -- 20+ games. School and Worldwide leaderboards showing top 100.

**Video Meetings** -- In-app video calls for conferences and staff meetings.

**Messaging System** -- Direct and group messaging. Real-time. Secure.

**AAC Talker Board** -- 200+ emoji cards with text-to-speech for special needs students.

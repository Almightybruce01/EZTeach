## Description (4000 characters max)
```
Stop juggling spreadsheets, paper, and disconnected tools. EZTeach unifies your entire school community—administrators, teachers, parents, and substitutes—in one secure, intuitive platform built for K–12.

WHAT SCHOOLS GET
• Grades and attendance with auto-calculation, weighted categories, and overrides
• School-wide calendar and announcements with audience filters
• Substitute booking with Sub Ranking—see who other schools trust, by school and city
• Bell schedules, documents, and school info visible to all
• District subscriptions: manage multiple schools under one payment

WHAT TEACHERS GET
• Enter grades, take attendance, create sub plans
• View rosters, classes, and students
• Direct messaging with parents
• Everything in one place

WHAT PARENTS GET
• Your child's grades and attendance—only your child, nothing else
• Linked by secure student code after selecting your school
• Announcements and messages from teachers
• Full transparency, zero clutter

WHAT SUBS GET
• View and accept sub assignments
• Build your profile across schools
• Get ranked by schools—compliments and complaints, tagged by school

WHAT MAKES EZTeach DIFFERENT
• Sub Ranking—top substitutes by school and city. No other platform has this.
• Funday Friday—every Friday you pick 6 rewards and spin to see which school wins
• Full parent integration with child-only secure access
• Districts pay for schools—no double billing
• Constant updates. Safe. Versatile. K–12. Public. Private.

FERPA-conscious. Encrypted. US-hosted. Built for educators.
Download EZTeach and connect your school.
```

## Description (4000 characters max)

EZTeach is the all-in-one K-12 school management platform that replaces 10+ separate tools with a single, beautiful app. Available on iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

**FOR SCHOOLS & ADMINISTRATORS**
• Student rosters, grades, GPA tracking, and attendance with analytics
• AI-powered lesson plans aligned to your state standards (CCSS, NGSS, TEKS, B.E.S.T., and more)
• Dynamic Standards System with national, state, district, and school-level overrides
• Searchable Standards Explorer with AI integration
• Homework assignments with photo and file submissions
• Video meetings with scheduling, codes, and in-app camera
• School-wide announcements from principals and teachers
• Bell schedules, calendar events, and emergency alerts
• Behavior tracking, student write-ups, and discipline management
• Document storage and management
• Bus route tracking and lunch menu management
• School library management with check-in/out, ISBN support, and overdue alerts
• Substitute teacher management with requests, approvals, rankings, and availability
• Default password reports and analytics dashboards

**FOR TEACHERS**
• AI Lesson Plans that generate standards-aligned plans in seconds
• Manage multiple grades and classrooms
• Homeroom, Roster, and Lesson Plan pages created automatically for each class
• Grade assignments with weighted categories and auto-calculated GPAs
• Direct messaging with parents, staff, and administrators
• Sub plans and notes for substitute teachers

**FOR PARENTS**
• Parent Portal — link via student code to see grades, homework, and attendance
• AI Study Plans — personalized learning paths for your child
• Submit homework photos and files directly
• Message teachers and staff
• Bus tracking and lunch menus
• Video meeting access for parent-teacher conferences

**FOR STUDENTS**
• EZLearning Games — 20+ educational games with math, reading, science, and more
• Leaderboards with My School and Worldwide views showing top 100 students
• Free books from Project Gutenberg — picture books, chapter books, and classics
• Multiplayer "Reading Together" sessions
• 120+ educational movies — nature, science, history, classic cinema, and more
• Electives hub for Art, Music, Dance, Band, and P.E.
• AAC Talker Board with 200+ emoji cards, categories, and text-to-speech
• Interactive picture books with tap-to-discover features

**PRICING**
EZTeach is free to download. Schools subscribe starting at $129/month — all features included at every tier. Tiers differ only by student capacity (200 to 7,500 students). District pricing available. Subscriptions are managed on our website at ezteach.org.

**PRIVACY & COMPLIANCE**
• FERPA compliant
• COPPA compliant
• ADA & IEP accommodations supported
• No ads. No data selling. Ever.
